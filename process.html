The Process

From first message to launched site.

Exactly what working together looks like — every step, every timeline, every decision point.

01

The Inquiry.

No commitment · Takes 2 minutes

It all starts with the inquiry form. You'll share the basics about your business, what you're looking for, your budget range, and your timeline. The more detail you give, the better prepared I'll be when we talk.

I'll review every inquiry personally and respond within 48 hours if it's a fit. If it's not, I'll let you know and try to point you toward someone who can help.

02

The Intro Call.

Free · 15 minutes · Phone or Zoom

A quick call to make sure we're a good fit. We'll cover what you're building at a high level, which package suits your scope, your timeline, and how the rest of the process works.

This isn't where we go deep on your audience, copy, or visual direction — that's saved for the Strategy Call after you book. The intro is about making sure we're aligned before anyone commits.

What we'll cover

The package that fits your project, your timeline, an overview of how I work, and any questions you have. Quick, friendly, and clear.

03

The Deposit + Assets.

50% deposit · One Drive folder · Required before strategy call

Once we agree to work together, I'll send over a contract and an invoice for 50% of your package total. Both need to be signed and paid before your Strategy Call is scheduled.

At the same time, you'll upload everything I need into one shared Google Drive folder: photos, logo files, written content, brand colors if you have them, links to anything that's inspiring you. Everything I'll need to build the site, in one place.

This is intentional — having everything in one folder means nothing gets lost across email threads and messages, and we both have a clean record of what was provided when.

04

The Strategy Call.

Up to 60 minutes · The deep dive

Now we go deep. This is the call where we build the real plan for your site — page by page, section by section, decision by decision.

You'll have already filled out my interactive Consultation Quiz (I'll send you the link with a private password) so we use the call to walk through your actual choices, not start from scratch. The Consultation lets you pre-select things like:

  • Visual style direction (modern, classic, editorial, etc.)
  • Site features (galleries, video, forms, integrations)
  • Color palette preferences
  • Typography direction
  • What pages you want and what each one needs to communicate

I also have a prep guide walking you through how to get the most out of this call. It's worth a read before we meet.

Your Consultation answers get emailed to both of us — so we have a paper-trail of every decision before I start building.

05

The Build.

~3 weeks · Heads-down work

This is where I disappear and build. For about 3 weeks, I'm in the work — designing, writing copy, building out every page, getting the photos right, making sure it works on every screen size.

I won't ping you constantly during this phase. Trust the process — you gave me everything in the Drive folder and on the Strategy Call, and the next time you'll see the site is when it's ready for your first review.

Important

The 3-week build window starts once your deposit and all assets are received. If anything is missing, the clock starts when everything is in.

06

The First Review.

7 days · Your turn

I'll send you a private preview link to the full site. You get 7 days to review it from top to bottom and submit your feedback in writing through my Feedback page (I'll send you a private password).

The Feedback page asks structured questions about each section — flow, copy, layout, photo choices — so your notes come back organized rather than scattered across emails. We'll have a follow-up call after to walk through it together, but the written notes are what I work from.

Round 1 feedback should focus on structural and flow changes. This is the round to tell me "this section is in the wrong place," "the copy here doesn't land," "this needs to be its own page." Big-picture stuff.

If you need more time

If 7 days isn't enough, just let me know before the window closes and we'll work out an extended timeline together. Going past the window without communication moves you down in the project queue.

07

The Revisions.

~2 weeks · Refinement

I take your written feedback and apply it. This typically takes 2 weeks depending on the scope of the changes.

Your package includes two rounds of revisions. Round 1 is the structural round we just covered. Round 2 is for refinement, polish, and small tweaks — not for total overhauls. If you decide you want to redesign the whole homepage in Round 2, that's a new scope and new pricing.

08

The Second Review.

7 days · Round 2

Same process as the first review — preview link, 7 days, structured feedback through the Feedback page, follow-up call. But this round is for refinement only: typos, alignment tweaks, photo refinements, and small copy tweaks.

09

The Final Edits + Handoff.

Final 50% due · Domain connection · Site goes live

I apply your Round 2 notes, then send the final invoice for the remaining 50%. Once it's paid, we connect your domain, push the site live, and walk through how to manage the parts you can update yourself — photos, links, banners.

From there, you own it. The site is yours. If you ever need help with maintenance, add-ons, or a refresh down the road, you can always reach back out — see The Extension for ongoing support options.
