The Process

From first message to launched site.

Here's exactly what working together looks like — every step, every timeline, every decision point. No surprises.

01

The Inquiry.

No commitment · Takes 2 minutes

It all starts with the inquiry form. You'll share the basics about your business, what you're looking for, your budget range, and your timeline. The more detail you give, the better prepared I'll be when we talk.

I'll review every inquiry personally and respond within 48 hours if it's a fit. If it's not, I'll let you know that too — and I'll always try to point you toward someone who can help.

02

The Intro Call.

Free · 15 minutes · Phone or Zoom

A quick call to get on the same page. We'll go over what you're building, what package you're considering, what your timeline looks like, and any questions you have about how I work.

This call is the chance for both of us to make sure we're a good match before anyone commits. No pressure, no pitch — just clarity.

What we'll cover

What you're trying to communicate, who your audience is, the scope you're imagining, your visual direction, and how the rest of the process works.

03

The Deposit + Assets.

50% deposit · One Drive folder · Before strategy

Once we agree to work together, I'll send over a contract and an invoice for 50% of your package total. Both need to be signed and paid before we move forward.

At the same time, you'll upload everything I need into one shared Google Drive folder: photos, logo files, written content, brand colors if you have them, links to anything that's inspiring you. Everything I'll need to build the site, in one place — not piecemeal over text and email.

This is important: I don't start the build until the deposit is paid and all your assets are in the folder. That way I can give the project my full attention without chasing you for things.

04

The Strategy Call.

Up to 60 minutes · The deep dive

Now we go deep. This is the call where we build the real plan for your site — page by page, section by section, decision by decision.

You'll have already filled out my interactive Consultation page (I'll send you the link with a private password) so we can use the call to walk through your actual choices, not start from scratch. The Consultation lets you pre-select things like:

  • Visual style direction (modern, classic, editorial, etc.)
  • Site features (galleries, video, forms, integrations)
  • Color palette preferences
  • Typography direction
  • What pages you want and what each one needs to communicate

Your selections get emailed to both of us automatically — so we'll have a paper trail of every decision before I start building.

05

The Build.

~3 weeks · Heads-down work

This is where I disappear and build. For about 3 weeks, I'm in the work — designing, writing copy, building out every page, getting the photos right, making sure it works on every screen size.

I won't ping you constantly during this phase. I'll send a quick check-in halfway through with a sneak peek if there's something I want your eyes on, but otherwise: trust the process. You gave me everything in the Drive folder and on the Strategy Call — now let me cook.

Important

The 3-week build window starts the day after the Strategy Call, assuming all assets were in the Drive folder when the deposit cleared. If assets come in late, the build clock starts when they're complete.

06

The First Review.

7 days · Your turn

I'll send you a private preview link to the full site. You get 7 days to review it from top to bottom and submit your feedback in writing through my Feedback page (I'll send you a private password).

The Feedback page asks you specific, structured questions about each section — flow, copy, layout, photo choices — so your notes come back organized rather than scattered across emails. We'll have a quick follow-up call after to walk through it together, but the written notes are what I work from.

Round 1 feedback should focus on structural and flow changes. This is the round to tell me "this section is in the wrong place," "the copy here doesn't land," "this needs to be its own page." Big-picture stuff.

If you need more time

If 7 days isn't enough, just let me know before the window closes and we'll work out an extended timeline together. Going past the window without communication moves you down in the project queue.

07

The Revisions.

~2 weeks · Refinement

I take your written feedback and apply it. This typically takes 2 weeks depending on the scope of the changes.

Your package includes two rounds of revisions. Round 1 is the structural round we just covered. Round 2 — coming up next — is for refinement, polish, and small tweaks. Round 2 is not for total overhauls. If you decide you want to redesign the whole homepage in Round 2, that's a new scope and new pricing.

08

The Second Review.

7 days · Round 2

Same process as the first review — preview link, 7 days, structured feedback through the Feedback page, follow-up call. But this round is for refinement only: typo fixes, photo swaps, color adjustments, small copy tweaks, alignment nudges.

09

The Final Edits + Handoff.

Final 50% due · Domain connection · Site goes live

I apply your Round 2 notes, then send the final invoice for the remaining 50%. Once it's paid, we connect your domain, push the site live, and I walk you through how to manage the parts you can update yourself — photos, links, banners, that kind of thing.

From there, you own it. The site is yours. If you ever need help with maintenance, add-ons, or a refresh down the road, you can always reach back out — see The Extension for ongoing support options.
